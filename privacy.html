Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last updated: December 2024

Simple Promise: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. This app works entirely on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information, including:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage patterns or app analytics
  • Photos, files, or other personal content
  • Device information or technical data

How Our Apps Work

Our applications operate entirely on your device. Any data you create or interact with stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We don't use frameworks that could potentially collect data about you.

App Store

Please note that downloading our app from the Apple App Store is subject to Apple's own privacy policy and terms of service, which are separate from ours.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or risk of data breaches from our end. Your information remains private and secure on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any information, we don't knowingly collect information from children under 13 or any other age group.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our approach to data collection (which is unlikely), we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app or our website.
