Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me

Last updated: December 2024

Simple Promise: I don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. My apps work entirely on your device.

Information I Don't Collect

My apps are designed with privacy in mind. I do not collect any personal information, including:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage patterns or app analytics
  • Photos, files, or other personal content
  • Device information or technical data

How My Apps Work

My applications operate entirely on your device. Any data you create or interact with stays on your device and is not transmitted to my servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

My apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. I don't use frameworks that could potentially collect data about you.

App Store

Please note that downloading my app from the Apple App Store is subject to Apple's own privacy policy and terms of service, which are separate from mine.

Data Security

Since I don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or risk of data breaches from my end. Your information remains private and secure on your device.

Children's Privacy

My apps are safe for users of all ages. Since I don't collect any information, I don't knowingly collect information from children under 13 or any other age group.

Changes to This Policy

If I ever change my approach to data collection (which is unlikely), I will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app or my website.
